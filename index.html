Privacy Policy — Daily Meals

Last updated: May 17, 2026

Daily Meals is a meal planning app for iOS developed by Łukasz Chochel (chochelstudio@gmail.com).

Data We Collect

Daily Meals does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. All information you enter — recipes, meal plans, shopping lists — is stored exclusively on your device using local storage. No data is sent to any server.

Third-Party Services

Daily Meals does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Children's Privacy

Daily Meals does not collect any data from any user, including children under 16.

Changes to This Policy

If the app's data practices change in the future (e.g. cloud sync, user accounts), this policy will be updated before those features are released.
